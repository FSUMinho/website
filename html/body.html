Body

Our role

The body sector will take care of everything related to bodywork, aerodynamics, suspension and chassis. It's up to us to deliver a strong and light structure that is both able to handle the abuse on track and to keep the driver safe in case of impact, to produce aerodynamic compenents that keep the car stable at high speeds and to develop a suspension able to keep the wheels in contact with the track at all times.
