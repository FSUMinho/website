Why invest in FSUM

We are more than a group of students with a pation for motorsport. FSUM represents the first step in the formation of top engineers. Our members will enter the job market with experience and skills that will make them stand out.
We want to offer students the chance of being part of group that values everyone's work and pushes all members to show their full potencial.
Investing in our project means investing in the next generation of engineers and making shure that thy gather as much experience as possible so that some day in the future they make a diference and push technology foward.

How to invest

Monetary support: Motorsport isn't cheap at all. Expences start accumulating in the development of the car and can reach a great ammount when we start competing. Supporting financially our project helps us menage all the expences all reach our goals.
Supply materials: In order to build the car that we are designing, we'll need to gather various materials. Supplying materials to the teams helps us take the car from the modeling software to the race track.
knowledge: Even though we consider ourselves very skilled and talented, knowledge is never enough. We are always open to learn from actual profetionals from our areas of expertise.
