The competition

Formula Student is divided in 3 classes.
Class 2 requires the presentation of a project and a prototype.
Class 1 will require a dynamic presentation of a car
equiped with an internal combustion engine.
Class 1A challenges teams to build a car
moved by an alternative source of energy.
The team aims to participate in the 2 and 1a classes.

Commitment with
the planet

We are commited to a greener future. That's why we chose
to equip our car with an electric power unit.
The team will also support a circular economy, promoting
the reuse of electrical components from portuguese industries
in order to reduce e-waste.
