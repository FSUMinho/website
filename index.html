About us

We are a group of engineering students from the University of Minho based in the city of Guimarães (Azurém Campus) with a pation for motorsport. As the latest addition to the Formula Student scene in Portugal, we aim to deliver a project with a solid foundation and, in the short term, become a reference in the north of the country.

Why Formula Student

Formula Student gives us students the chance to get a taste of the world of engineering. Building our car requires us to apply all the knowledge we have about our areas of expertise and work with parteners that, besides supporting our project, can help us improve our methods. This project also aims to develop soft skills like menagement, communication and team work, all crucial in a top tier engineer.

The competition

Formula Student is divided in 3 classes:
Class 2 requires the presentation of a project and a prototype.
Class 1 will require a dynamic presentation of a car equiped with an internal combustion engine.
Class 1A challenges teams to build a car moved by an alternative source of energy.
The team aims to participate in the 2 and 1A classes.

Commitment with
the planet

We are commited to a greener future. That's why we chose to equip our car with an electric power unit.
The team will also support a circular economy, promoting the reuse of electrical components from portuguese industries in order to reduce e-waste. At least 25% of electronic "trash" that reaches recycling centers, still is higly reusable. This materials are fundamental to reduce our carbon footprint.
